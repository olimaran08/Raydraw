What is NATA and who should take it?

+

NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) is conducted by the Council of Architecture to assess aptitude for B.Arch programs. Any student in Class 12 or above who wants to study architecture in India must clear NATA. It tests drawing, spatial reasoning, and mathematics.

How long does it take to crack NATA?

+

With dedicated coaching, 3–6 months is ideal for most students. If you’re starting from scratch with no drawing background, 6 months is recommended. Students with some foundation can achieve strong scores in 3 months with our Intensive program.

Do you offer online NATA coaching?

+

Yes. We offer live online coaching via video sessions with the same curriculum and personal drawing critique as our in-person batches. Students from across Tamil Nadu and India attend our online programs.

What is the fee and is EMI available?

+

The Crash Course starts at ₹18,000, Intensive at ₹24,000, and Foundation at ₹38,000. EMI options are available for all programs from ₹6,400/month. No hidden charges — ever.

Do I need prior drawing experience for NATA?

+

No prior drawing experience is required. Our Foundation program is designed specifically for complete beginners. Drawing aptitude is a skill — it can be taught and developed with the right guidance. Many of our 130+ scorers started with zero drawing background.

Can parents visit before enrolling?

Absolutely — we encourage it. Come in, meet the founders, and sit in on a trial class before making any commitment. Call or WhatsApp to schedule a visit at a time that suits you.
